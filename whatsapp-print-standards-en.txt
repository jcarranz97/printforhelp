🖨️ *Important when printing!* 🖨️

Hi! The people in Venezuela are asking us to please follow the *printing
standards*: use the *correct filament* and the *correct settings* on
every part. 💚

This way we make sure each print truly helps those who need it. We
gathered all the information on PrintForHelp so it is easy to follow and
to share. 🖨️

👉 https://printforhelp.org/#print-standards

🙏 If you can, *share the link* so more makers keep it in mind.
*We are counting on your solidarity!* 💜
